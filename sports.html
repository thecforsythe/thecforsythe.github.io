Zombie Shuffle

One Million computer programers participated in this years Zombie Shuffle, which took place on Saturday, to raise awarness for programing adiction, which claimed more than 3,000 lives this year in the Eastern Delta Area. This year the race was sponsored by Treyarch. A full stack programer named Dustin Yelland won the shuffle in a time of 2 hours 11 minutes.

Quote by Treyarch: "When the shaking stops, look around. If there is a clear path to safety, leave the building and go to an open space away from damaged areas."
